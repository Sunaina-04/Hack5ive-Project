Hydration & Nourishment

Hydration Habits

  • Drink Water Regularly: Aim for at least 8 glasses of water a day.
  • Carry a Water Bottle: Make it easy to stay hydrated throughout the day.
  • Infuse Your Water: Add fruits like lemon, cucumber, or berries for flavor.
  • Consume Water-Rich Foods: Include watermelon, cucumbers, and oranges in your diet.
  • Limit Sugary Drinks: Opt for water or unsweetened beverages instead.
  • Drink Before, During, and After Exercise: Replenish lost fluids.

Nourishment Habits

  • Eat a Balanced Diet: Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
  • Prioritize Whole Foods: Minimize processed foods and sugary snacks.
  • Consume Healthy Fats: Include avocados, nuts, and olive oil in your diet.
  • Eat Mindfully: Pay attention to your hunger and fullness cues.
  • Include Protein in Every Meal: Helps with satiety and muscle maintenance.
  • Limit Salt and Sugar Intake: Reduces the risk of chronic diseases.
  • Don't Skip Breakfast: Kickstart your metabolism and energy levels.
  • Eat plenty of fiber: helps with digestion and satiety.
